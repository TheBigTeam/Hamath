How To Play

Hamath is a game that is created to help you in you math classes. We hope that you will enjoy it as much as possible!

In order you to enjoy it, here is a list of tips how to play the game.

  • Do not let the enemies touch the earth.
  • Give the right answer for the math problem in order to pass in the second level.
  • Use the mouse for moving around the enemies.
  • Use the keybord for numbers
  • Be aware that with the speed of the enemies increases while you go to higher levels of difficulties.
